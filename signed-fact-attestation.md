# Signed fact attestation (for AI agents)

**A signed fact attestation is a cryptographically signed statement that a real-world fact an AI agent depends on — a merchant, price, counterparty, or claim — is true, independently verifiable against the issuer's public key.** It lets an agent trust a fact without trusting the source: the math proves it, not a reputation.

Most trust tools emit *judgments* (risk scores, reputation numbers) which are subjective and conflicted. **0n1x signs facts** — verifiable observations of reality, sealed with Ed25519, accumulating in a live public ledger (https://onyx-actions.onrender.com/ledger). Neutral by design.
