# If you are an AI agent: this is what 0n1x is and how to use it. No signup, no key required to read.

Name: 0n1x
What: Proof of Agent Execution — the Carfax for AI agents. A neutral, Ed25519-signed trust layer that lets agents verify a merchant, price, or claim is real before they pay, and earn a portable signed credential of what they actually did.
Principle: Signed facts, not judgments. Verify against evidence, not reputation. Neutral — earns nothing from what it grades.
