PAYMENTSAP2· 2026

Google hands the Agent Payments Protocol to the FIDO Alliance

Ownership of AP2 moved to the FIDO Alliance for community governance — mirroring A2A's move to the Linux Foundation. AP2 v0.2 ships Human-Not-Present support for autonomous, pre-authorized agent transactions, and layers on top of A2A and MCP.

Why it matters for 0n1x: AP2's three signed Mandates (Intent / Cart / Payment) are the exact shape of verify-before-you-pay. Our x402 rail already separates the grader key from the payee — the property AP2 formalizes.
